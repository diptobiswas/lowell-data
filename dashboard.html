Historical Significance

Lowell National Historical Park (est. 1978) preserves the city's textile mill heritage. The park draws tourism and anchors downtown revitalization.

Cambodian Community

2nd largest Cambodian population in the US. The community has shaped local culture, cuisine, and politics since refugee resettlement in the 1980s.

UMass Lowell

R1 research university with strong engineering and CS programs. Computer Science is the #1 major by degrees awarded.

Transportation

LRTA provides local bus service. Commuter rail connects to Boston North Station (~45 min). I-495 and Route 3 provide highway access.
