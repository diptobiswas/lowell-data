Critical: Governance & Tech Radar at L1
The April 2023 ransomware attack demonstrates policies weren't embedded in workflows ‚Äî a L1 trait ("reactive compliance"). No tech radar exists at all, falling below the L2 requirement of "simple radar with Adopt/Trial/Hold."

Bright Spots: Strategy & Metrics at L3

The L2 Plateau
Most dimensions sit at L2: "Leaders sponsor digitization to cut cost." Lowell has shared tools (Tyler, ESRI), basic dashboards, and upskilling beginning ‚Äî but lacks the cross-functional teams, API standards, and enterprise data programs needed for L3.

üí° Priority Actions to Reach Level 3

  1. Governance Remediation (L1‚ÜíL2): Embed security policies in workflows. Leverage UMass Lowell SOC. Implement compliance-by-design, not reactive audits.
  2. Create a Tech Radar (L1‚ÜíL2): Document a simple Adopt/Trial/Hold/Retire framework for technology decisions. Update annually. Tie procurement to the radar.

  4. Cross-Functional Teams (L2‚ÜíL3): Pilot 2-3 product teams with named Service Owners. Embed tech/data/design in teams, not as separate departments.
